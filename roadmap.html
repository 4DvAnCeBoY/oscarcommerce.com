Welcome to the Oscar Framework Roadmap! Here you will find a quick look at our product priorities over the next 12 months.

Search refactor

Planned

Oscar's search app will be rewritten to support faceting and autocomplete as standard.

Payflow Pro support for PayPal

In Progress 75%

Oscar's PayPal extension is being expanded to support Payflow Pro. This allows PayPal to be treated as a payment gateway without requiring the customer to be redirected to the PayPal site.

GoCardless extension

In Progress 60%

A new Oscar extension is in preparation that integrates with the GoCardless payment gateway.

Improved demo site

In Progress 30%

The current demo site is a vanilla build of Oscar's master branch. A better demo site is in preparation that demonstrates handling of a range of product types and has a fully fleshed out checkout.

Digital product support

Completed

Oscar 0.3 will include better support for digital products, allowing purchasing and checkout to be streamlined.
